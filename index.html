Our Story
Born in a Village Kitchen,
Made for Your Table
Marihan, Mirzapur, UP Where every jar is made with love

Achaar Adda was born in a small home kitchen in Marihan village, Mirzapur — surrounded by mango trees, fields of mustard, and the smell of sizzling spices that has filled this home for generations.

We don't use machines or factories. Every jar is hand-packed, every masala is freshly ground, and every pickle is made exactly the way our family has made it for decades — slowly, carefully, and with complete love.

When you open a jar of Achaar Adda, you're tasting real ghar ka khana. The bold tang of raw mango. The deep heat of Mirzapur's red chilli. The earthiness of jackfruit marinated in kachi ghani mustard oil. That's what we put in every jar.

FAQ
Questions? We've Got Answers
Are your pickles really preservative-free? How do they last?
▼
Yes, absolutely! We use zero artificial preservatives. Pure mustard oil, salt, and natural spices are themselves powerful natural preservatives — this is exactly how pickles were made for thousands of years before chemical preservatives existed. Our pickles last 12 months unopened and 6 months after opening, stored in a cool dry place.

Is Achaar Adda FSSAI registered? Is it safe to eat?
▼
Yes! Achaar Adda is fully registered with the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI). Our FSSAI registration number is printed on every jar. Our home kitchen follows all food safety and hygiene standards required by law. You can eat our pickles with complete confidence.

For bulk orders, corporate gifting, or reselling inquiries, please contact us directly on WhatsApp at +91 93368 22660 and we'll create a custom quote for you.
How should I store my Achaar Adda pickle after opening?
▼
Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Always use a clean, dry spoon — never a wet one, as moisture can spoil pickles. After opening, refrigerate for best freshness and consume within 6 months. Make sure the oil layer always covers the pickle pieces to keep them fresh longer.
